The DevWrites Project

The DevWrites project was developed by Shodipo O. John and Lijadu Mofeoluwa as part of the #30DaysOfFlask Challenge.

Users will create accounts and have profiles, they can write, edit and delete their articles, they can also read and comment on articles from other users. Users can follow and unfollow other users to see their articles. Users are able to update their profiles or delete their accounts, they can search and find other users on the Find Writers page.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, BOOTSTRAP, FLASK(backend) and SQLlite(database).

However, you can contribute to the DevWrites project. Link to the GitHub repostory: The DevWrites Project.
